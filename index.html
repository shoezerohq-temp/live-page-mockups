Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about custom shoes, ordering, and shipping

Q1 How much does customization cost?

Nothing extra. Customization is free. The price on the product page is the full price — no setup fees, no design fees, no hidden charges.

Q2 Will the print fade or peel over time?

Not under normal wear. The print bonds directly into the shoe material, so it won't crack, peel, or fade the way a surface-level print would.

Q3 What file formats can I upload?

We accept PNG, JPG, and SVG files. For the sharpest print, use a PNG with a transparent background at 300 DPI or higher.

Q4 Which parts of the shoe can I customize?

You can customize the outer panels, inner panels, tongue, heel, toe box, insole, and even the shoe box. Our design tool highlights each section.

Q5 What is your return policy?

Since every pair is custom printed, we can't accept returns for sizing. Check the size chart on each product page before ordering. If you run into an issue, reach out and we'll work with you.
