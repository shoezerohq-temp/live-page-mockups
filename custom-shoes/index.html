See our manufacturing process

Designs printed into the material

Direct-to-garment printed before the panels are cut. Pre-treatment is tuned per substrate so colors stay vivid and the print does not fade.

Laser-cut precision

After printing, a CO2 laser cuts each panel from the material. The cut follows the design's exact borders and seals the edges.

Hand-assembled, machine-sewn

Industrial sewing machines stitch the panels into the upper one at a time, every seam guided by hand. The upper is then bonded to the sole through cemented construction.

Quality checked, laced, shipped

Each pair gets a visual inspection before shipping. The team reviews print alignment, color accuracy, stitching, and topcoat coverage.

Why Choose Custom Shoes?

Each shoe style built for a different person and a different purpose.

Best For

People order Shoe Zero for personal projects, branded merchandise, and everything in between.

  • Artists & designers turning their artworks into wearable footwear
  • Businesses who want their logo on the shoes and marketing events gear
  • Schools, teams, & organizations ordering custom shoes in bulk
  • Parties & events like weddings and trade shows that need matching shoes
  • Gift-givers designing something personal that people actually wear

Materials & Quality

  • Synthetic leather (PU). Polyurethane uppers that are water-resistant, easy to clean, and completely animal-free
  • Canvas. Woven fabric that holds printed designs with sharp, clean detail
  • Mesh and flyknit. Lightweight, breathable uppers built for running and training shoes
  • EVA cushioned insoles. Lightweight foam padding across all styles for shock absorption and day-long comfort. Removable on most models
  • Rubber outsoles. Wear-resistant soles on most styles for grip and durability
  • Machine washable. Most styles go in the wash without damaging the printed design

Find Your Style

Six shoe types, each built for different activities.

  • Low-Tops. The go-to for everyday wear and branded footwear, from synthetic leather to mesh to canvas
  • High-Tops. Extra panels and more printable space for your design
  • Basketball Shoes. Court-ready styles with cushioned midsoles and ankle support
  • Boots. Synthetic leather that is waterproof with rubber outsoles and foam-lined interiors
  • Kids Shoes. Toddler to early teen sizes in canvas and synthetic leather
  • Sandals. Slides, flip flops, and slippers built for summer and indoor comfort
